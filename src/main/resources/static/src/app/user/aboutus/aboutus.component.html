About Us

Peachkite brings together both women and organizations on to a single platform. to offer women career support initiatives and receive feedback.

Organizations, who respect the right of women to have a career on her terms and provide an opportunity for her to fulfill both her career aspirations and social obligations with equal ease

Peachkite brings together women to assist each other in uplifting their career, identify the most appropriate workplace and provide genuine & anonymous feedback to organisations for them to tap the right talent and gain an insight to the effectiveness of their initiatives.

So, come and fly your kite.
